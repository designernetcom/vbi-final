About Us

Charged with Purpose to Shaping Future of Energy

Established with a vision to deliver excellence in electrical solutions, we at VBI, specialize in the manufacturing of cable glands, copper and aluminium terminals (lugs), and other earthing accessories. Over the years, we have expanded our offerings to include a comprehensive inventory of reputed industrial electrical products and control panel accessories.
